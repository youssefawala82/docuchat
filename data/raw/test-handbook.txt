Company Handbook — Nebula Robotics Inc.

About the Company
Nebula Robotics Inc. was founded in 2019 by Elena Vasquez and Tom Okafor in
Austin, Texas. The company builds autonomous warehouse robots used by
logistics companies to move inventory between storage shelves and packing
stations. As of 2026, Nebula Robotics employs 340 people across three
offices: Austin (headquarters), Berlin, and Singapore.

Product Line
The company's flagship product is the "Skimmer" robot, a low-profile
autonomous cart that slides under shelving units and lifts them for
transport. The second-generation Skimmer, released in 2023, increased
battery life from 6 hours to 14 hours per charge and added obstacle
detection using LIDAR sensors. Nebula also sells "Nebula Fleet Manager,"
a software dashboard that lets warehouse operators track and schedule
robots in real time.

Vacation Policy
Full-time employees accrue 18 days of paid vacation per year during their
first three years of employment. After three years, this increases to 23
days per year. Vacation days do not roll over between calendar years,
except for up to 5 unused days, which may be carried into the next year
with manager approval. Part-time employees accrue vacation on a pro-rated
basis according to their contracted hours.

Remote Work Policy
Employees in engineering, design, and customer success roles may work
remotely up to 3 days per week, subject to manager approval. Employees
in manufacturing, hardware testing, and on-site logistics roles are
required to work on-site five days per week due to the physical nature
of their work. All remote work requests must be submitted through the
HR portal at least one week in advance.

Expense Reimbursement
Employees may submit expense reports for approved business travel,
client meetings, and necessary equipment purchases. Reimbursement
requests must be submitted within 30 days of the expense and require a
valid receipt. Expenses over $500 require pre-approval from a direct
manager before the purchase is made. Reimbursements are typically
processed within 10 business days of approval.

Performance Reviews
Nebula Robotics conducts formal performance reviews twice per year, in
March and September. Reviews are based on goals set collaboratively
between the employee and their manager during the previous review cycle.
Salary adjustments, when applicable, are typically implemented following
the September review cycle, effective the following January.
